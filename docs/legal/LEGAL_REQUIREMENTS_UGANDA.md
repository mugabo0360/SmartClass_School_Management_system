# SmartClass Legal Requirements Checklist (Uganda)

> Not legal advice. Use this as a practical startup checklist and confirm with a lawyer/accountant.

## 1) Business Setup
- Register business name/company with URSB.
- Get TIN from URA.
- Keep contracts/invoices for every school.

## 2) Data Protection
- Align operations with Uganda Data Protection and Privacy law/principles.
- Publish privacy policy and terms publicly.
- Collect explicit consent checkboxes at signup.
- Maintain access controls and data export/deletion process.

## 3) Contracts
- School service agreement (scope, term, pricing, uptime target).
- Data Processing Addendum (controller/processor roles).
- Refund and cancellation terms.

## 4) Payments
- Define accepted channels (MTN, Airtel, bank).
- Keep payment reconciliation records.
- Add clear refund policy.

## 5) Student Data Handling
- Collect minimum data necessary.
- Encrypt data in transit and at rest.
- Restrict access by school and role.
- Keep incident response process for breaches.

## 6) Marketing Compliance
- Send WhatsApp/SMS only to opted-in contacts.
- Include stop/unsubscribe instruction in campaigns.
- Keep opt-in evidence.

## 7) Minimum Documentation to Keep
- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Refund Policy
- Data Processing Addendum
- Incident response contact and process
